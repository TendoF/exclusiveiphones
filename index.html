Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before placing your order.

Are the products genuinely brand new and not refurbished?
Absolutely. Every single device we sell is 100% brand new and factory sealed in its original box — straight from the supplier to you. We do not sell refurbished, repaired, or second-hand devices under any circumstances. You will receive your product in the exact same condition it left the factory, complete with all original accessories and manufacturer warranty.
How do I place an order?
Ordering is simple. You can either add a product to your cart on this website and checkout via WhatsApp, or contact us directly on WhatsApp at +27 83 868 7662. Tell us which device you want, confirm your delivery address, and make payment via EFT, bank transfer, or Cash on Delivery (where available). Once payment is confirmed, we dispatch your order within 24 hours.
How long does delivery take and how much does it cost?
We offer nationwide delivery across South Africa via Postnet, PEP, and The Courier Guy. Standard delivery takes 1–2 business days to most areas. Delivery fees are calculated based on your location and will be confirmed when you place your order via WhatsApp. For orders in certain high-demand areas, we may also offer same-day collection options — ask us when you order.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept EFT (Electronic Funds Transfer), direct bank deposits, Visa and Mastercard payments, and Cash on Delivery in select areas. Cash on Delivery availability depends on your location — please WhatsApp us before ordering to confirm. We do not accept partial payments or deposits for COD orders.
Is Cash on Delivery available in my area?
Cash on Delivery is available in select areas, primarily across Gauteng and parts of other major provinces. To confirm whether COD is available for your area, please send us a WhatsApp message with your location before placing your order. We'll confirm immediately. Please note that for COD orders, a small commitment deposit may be required before dispatch.
Can I return or exchange a product if I'm not happy?
We stand behind every product we sell. If your device arrives damaged, defective, or is not the product you ordered, we will arrange a replacement or full refund. Returns must be reported within 48 hours of delivery via WhatsApp, with photos or a video of the issue. Please note that returns for buyer's remorse (changing your mind about a model) are not accepted, so please confirm your choice carefully before ordering.
How do I know this business is legitimate and trustworthy?
We have served over 15,000 customers across South Africa and built our reputation entirely on trust and transparency. Visit our Instagram page @exclusiveiphonesrsa to see hundreds of real delivery photos, unboxing videos, and reviews from verified customers. We encourage you to do your research before ordering — our track record speaks for itself. You can also WhatsApp us at any time and we'll answer all your questions honestly.
Do you offer warranty on devices? What happens if my device has a fault?
All brand new devices come with manufacturer warranty as included in the original box. If you experience a manufacturing defect after delivery, you can take the device to any authorised Apple service centre in South Africa for warranty claims. For any delivery or packaging-related issues, contact us directly on WhatsApp within 48 hours and we will resolve it personally and promptly.
